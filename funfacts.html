Fun Facts

Learn something new about your favorite movies and shows!

  • The original “Jurassic Park” dinosaurs were mostly animatronics, not CGI.
  • “Friends” was almost called “Insomnia Cafe.”
  • The Harry Potter movies used over 700 different wigs for extras and main characters.
  • “The Simpsons” holds the record for the longest-running animated TV series.
