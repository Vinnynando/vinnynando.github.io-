Game Rules

How to play:

  1. Look at the clue provided (poster, emoji, scene, or quote).
  2. Type your guess into the answer box.
  3. Click submit to see if you’re correct.
  4. If correct, you’ll see a fun fact and earn points!
  5. Try to guess as many as you can and climb the leaderboard!
